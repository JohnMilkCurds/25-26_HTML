Ordered and Unordered Lists

An unordered list uses the ul and li tags and formats with bullets. The ul tag declares the list type, and the li tags are the items

List of Shame

  • Josiah
  • Cam
  • Joseph

An ordered list uses the ol and li tags and formats with numbers. The ol tag declares the list type, and the li tags are the items

Hollow Knight any% Boss list

  1. False Knight
  2. Hornet 1
  3. Mantis Lords
  4. Uumuu
  5. Watcher Knights
  6. The Hollow Knight

Descriptive lists use dl, dt, and dd. Dl stands for descriptive list, dt stands for descriptive topic, and dd stands for descriptive data.
